## Tascam DA-6400 Multichannel Audio Recorder

This module will control the Tascam DA-6400.

- [Tascam DA 6400 Page](https://tascam.com/us/product/da-6400/)

## Configuration
Enter the IP address, port and password of the recorder.

## Actions
- **Stop** 
- **Play** 
- **Record** 
- **Pause** 
- **Search** 
- **Skip**
- **Repeat Mode**
- **Remote/Local Select**
- **Play Mode Select**
- **Mark Set**
